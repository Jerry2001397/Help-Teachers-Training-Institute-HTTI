Courses Offered

Teacher training designed for real classroom impact.

HTTI offers a balanced programme that combines education foundations, classroom practice, child development, ethics, and digital teaching skills to prepare confident and effective educators.

Training Areas

Core courses that build professional readiness

Each course is structured to strengthen subject knowledge, practical teaching ability, and the professional standards expected of effective teachers.

  • 🏫
    Education Foundations Foundations of Education

    Builds a strong understanding of the purpose, structure, and values of the teaching profession.

    Read more

    This course introduces the history of education, the role of schools in society, and the professional responsibilities expected of effective teachers.

  • 📚
    Subject Mastery Subject Contents

    Strengthens mastery of the subjects teachers are expected to teach with confidence and accuracy.

    Read more

    Learners deepen their content knowledge across key teaching areas so they can explain concepts clearly, answer questions well, and prepare effective lessons.

  • 🧒
    Learner Development Child Development

    Explores how children grow, learn, behave, and respond at different stages of development.

    Read more

    This course helps future teachers understand physical, emotional, social, and cognitive development so they can support learners more effectively.

  • 📝
    Teaching Practice Pedagogy

    Focuses on classroom teaching methods, lesson delivery, and strategies that improve learning outcomes.

    Read more

    Students learn how to plan instruction, manage classroom engagement, and apply teaching approaches suited to different learners and subjects.

  • 👤
    Professional Identity Teacher Profile

    Shapes the identity, ethics, habits, and leadership qualities expected from professional teachers.

    Read more

    The course emphasizes personal conduct, professionalism, communication, and the character traits that help teachers become trusted role models.

  • 🎓
    Classroom Experience Practice Teaching

    Gives trainees direct teaching experience in real classroom settings under supervision.

    Read more

    Learners apply lesson planning, class management, and instructional skills during practical teaching sessions and receive feedback for improvement.

  • 💻
    Digital Teaching Digital Education

    Introduces technology tools that support modern teaching, research, and classroom communication.

    Read more

    This course prepares teachers to use digital resources, online platforms, and educational technology responsibly and effectively in their work.

  • 🧸
    Early Years Education Early Childhood Training/Education

    Prepares teachers to guide the learning and care of young children during their earliest school years.

    Read more

    It covers age-appropriate teaching techniques, play-based learning, child safety, and classroom environments that support early growth and development.

  • ⚖️
    Ethics and Citizenship Moral/Ethical Education

    Promotes values, responsible behavior, and ethical decision-making in school and community life.

    Read more

    This course helps teachers model integrity, teach good citizenship, and encourage learners to make respectful and principled choices.
