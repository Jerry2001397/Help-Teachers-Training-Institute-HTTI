Online Library

The HTTI Online Library brings together teaching materials, reference texts, and digital learning resources to support study, lesson preparation, and continuous professional growth.

Available Resources

  • A
    Education Texts

    Core books and reading materials on pedagogy, curriculum, psychology, and classroom management.

  • B
    Lesson Planning Guides

    Samples, templates, and practical guides to help students and teachers prepare effective lessons.

  • C
    Digital Reference Materials

    Curated online materials that support research, assignments, presentations, and independent study.

Learning Support

The library is designed to make quality teaching resources easier to access for trainees, instructors, and partner schools looking for professional learning materials.
